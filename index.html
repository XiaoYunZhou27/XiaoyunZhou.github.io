About Me

I am a senior research scientist at PAII Inc. I received my PhD from Department of Computing, Imperial College London in 2020. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Guang-Zhong Yang, a pioneer in Medical Robotics. Before that, I received my master degree from Tsinghua University.

My research interests are mainly computer vision, deep learning, and medical image analysis and robotics.
